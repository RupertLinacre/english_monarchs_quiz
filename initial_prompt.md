This app is a monarchs of england quiz, starting 1066 to present.

You will need to go online and find an authoratitive source of English monarchs and their reigns.

It's like a sporcle quiz - it has an answer box and when you enter a correct answer it immediately accepts. As you go it tells you how many you got right like 1/42, 2/42 and so on.

Here's the difference with Sporcle: the quiz is based around an interactive zoomable timeline, presented horizontally running 1066 to present

When you get an answer right, the monarch's name appears above the timeline in the correct place with the correct dates in a segment.  Their picture shows above the segment.

Also shown above the timeline are the higher level periods, House of Normandy, Angevins and so on.

Below the timeline is shown (always shown even before anything answered) key historical events such as the battle of hastings.  Not many of them, one or two per ruler.

When you answer correctly, the timeline zooms into the correct place on the timeline.

You'll want some clever solution to preventing text overlapping when you zoom out too much.

I've scaffolding a blank vite typescript app.  You do the rest.
